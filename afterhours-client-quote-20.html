Terms of Service

Afterhours Office Cleaning · Please read before booking

1. Services

Afterhours Office Cleaning agrees to provide commercial cleaning services as selected during the quote process. Services are performed after business hours unless otherwise arranged. All work is completed by trained, professional staff.

2. Pricing & Payment

All quoted prices are flat-rate and all-inclusive. A 10% deposit is required to secure your booking. The deposit is applied toward your first visit. Remaining balance is due on the date of service. Invoices are sent via email after each visit.

3. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made at least 48 hours in advance will receive a full deposit refund. Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the scheduled visit are non-refundable. We reserve the right to reschedule due to emergencies or unsafe conditions.

4. Access to Premises

Client agrees to provide safe and legal access to the premises on each scheduled service date. Access codes, keys, or fobs must be provided via our secure Access Authorization Form — never by phone or unsecured text. Client is responsible for disabling and re-enabling any alarms.

5. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to secure or remove any sensitive documents, valuables, or fragile items prior to each service. Afterhours Office Cleaning is not liable for damage to improperly stored items.

6. Liability & Insurance

Afterhours Office Cleaning maintains general liability insurance. In the event of accidental damage caused by our staff, we must be notified within 24 hours of the service visit. We are not liable for pre-existing damage or conditions.

7. Satisfaction Guarantee

If you are unsatisfied with any area of your cleaning, notify us within 24 hours and we will return to correct it at no charge. Concerns reported after 24 hours cannot be attributed to that service visit.

8. Recurring Services

Recurring service agreements continue until cancelled by either party with at least 7 days written notice via email or text. Rate changes will be communicated at least 14 days in advance.

9. Photography

We may photograph completed work for quality assurance purposes. No photos will include sensitive documents, personal items, or identifiable information. Photos are never shared publicly without written consent.
