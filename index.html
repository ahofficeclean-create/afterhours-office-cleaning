Service Terms

Afterhours Office Cleaning — Please read before booking

Service Agreement

By submitting a booking request with Afterhours Office Cleaning, you agree to allow our team access to your office space at the scheduled time for the purpose of cleaning services.

Scheduling

All services are performed after hours, once your office has closed for the day. We will contact you within 24 hours of your request to confirm a start time that works for your schedule.

Payment

Payment is due after each completed cleaning visit. An invoice will be sent to your email on file. Recurring clients may set up automatic payments.

Cancellation Policy

We require 48 hours notice to cancel or reschedule a visit. Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Access & Security

You agree to provide accurate access instructions. Afterhours Office Cleaning will treat all access codes and security information as strictly confidential.

Satisfaction Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with a cleaning, notify us within 24 hours and we will return to correct the issue at no additional charge.
